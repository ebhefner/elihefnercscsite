- Outerbanks -

The Outerbanks is a 200-mile expanse of barrier islands full of impressive landscapes, beaches, dunes, towns, and lighthouses. There are a plethora of attractions found in the Outer Banks, ranging from beachside horse riding to shipwreck diving. Beyond that, there are a variety of museums found throughout the barrier islands, such as the Wright Brothers National Memorial. It is located on the historic location of their first flight, Kitty Hawk, and contains a replica of the Wright Brothers' first plane and various recreations of other fascinating historic elements surrounding the history of flight.
